Tartar Sauce

Source: incredibleegg.org adapted by Michael Pistrang and Maeve Pistrang

1 cup mayonnaise
2.5 T finely chopped sweet pickle
1.5 T finely chopped onion
1 T minced parsley
1 tsp prepared mustard

Combine all the ingredients in a bowl, mix well. Refrigerate, covered.

This tartar sauce is also great with any of the following ingredients
(as additions or replacements):
1 T capers
1 tsp dried dill
1/2 tsp cayenne
1/2 tsp paprika
